About:

Project Civics is an online advanced civics resource for U.S. citizens seeking a way to
understand the historical, political, and institutional information they need in order
to remain informed and follow current events. We emphasize visuals and interactive tools in order to make the learning experience more accessible.

Our goal is to explain all sides of an issue to our readers as if they
have never heard of this issue before and are new to the American political scene.
We are committed to addressing each complex civics concept and tough question in an
accessible and– as much as possible– unbiased way.
